`src/assets/img/icons/`.

- [`docs/licenses/phosphor-icons-MIT.txt`](./licenses/phosphor-icons-MIT.txt)

The SVGs were imported from a local copy at `B:\Downloads\phosphor-icons` and then integrated into
the project's automatic icon export workflow via `pnpm generate-icons`.
